Nutrition 101

Our food intake can be divided into two main categories: energy and essential nutrients.

"The carbohydrates, fats and proteins in food supply energy, which is measured in calories. Carbohydrates and proteins provide 4 calories per gram. Fat contributes more than twice as much -- 9 calories per gram -- and foods that are high in fat are also high in calories. Alcohol is also high in energy and supplies 7 calories per gram."1

Carbohydrates, fats, and proteins are our macronutrients. Since they are such a big part of our diet, it is important to consume them in the right ratios.
